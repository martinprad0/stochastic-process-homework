\section{Exercise 2}
Show that for every random variable $X \in L^2$,
\[ \E[\; |X-\E X|^2 \;]\leq \E [\; |X|^2 \;] . \]

\subsection*{Solution}
Since $|x|^2 = x^2$, it follows that,
\[ \everymath{\displaystyle}
\arraycolsep=1.8pt\def\arraystretch{1.8}
\begin{array}{rl}
    \E[\; |X-\E X|^2 \;] & = \E {(X-\E X)}^2\\
    & = \E[X^2] - E{[X]}^2\\
    & = \E[\; |X|^2 \;] - E{[X]}^2\\
    & \leq \E[\; |X|^2 \;] .
\end{array} \]
